About Us

OnionLens is intended to detect and differentiate healthy and unhealthy onion harvests. This technology uses innovative technology such as artificial intelligence, data processing, and image recognition to examine visual data gathered from onion farms. Through processing of this data, OnionLens is able to precisely identify the typical symptoms and patterns that indicate health or illness, enabling early identification of impacted plants.

With the help of OnionLens, farmers and agricultural workers could identify onion infections early, allowing them to employ effective management techniques and reduce the disease's economic impact on onion production.

Verify that the symptoms identified correspond to an armyworm infection. Armyworm damage can take several forms, including wilting, holes in leaves, and the appearance of larvae. Hand-remove and destroy the affected plants and caterpillars if the infestation is not too severe. Pheromone traps should be used to catch adult moths and lower the population. Neem oil or insecticidal soap are good options for organic gardening. Clear weeds and debris from areas where armyworms could hide or lay eggs. Refrain from overwatering as this may draw bugs.
